Virtual NIT-T

We are trying to convert our college(NIT Trichy) into virtual reality to give an awesome experience to the students who are missing the college during online semester.
Currently we've devloped Admin block and are planning to cover the entire campus and convert it into more of a virtual tour - more of like walking in the campus.
Excited?
ComeOn, Join us in making it real. We have used Blender to make models, and threeJS for rendering it over the site but will soon move over to Unity.
So, if you know Blender or Unity already, or even if you don't know but are willing to learn and contribute.
Connect with me over Instagram, or send a mail to priyanshudangipd@gmail.com.
Also, report any bug, problem or improvement over priyanshudangipd@gmail.com.
