Virtual NIT-T

We are trying to convert our college(NIT Trichy) into a virtual reality.
Currently its only Admin block, but we are planning to cover the full campus and convert it into more of a virtual tour(more of like walking in the campus).
Excited?
ComeOn, Join us in making it real. We have used Blender to make models, and threeJS for rendering it over site but will soon move over to Unity(as the project grows).
So, if you know Blender or Unity already, or even if you don't know but willing to learn and contribute. Connect with me over Instagram, or send a mail to priyanshudangipd@gmail.com .
Also, report any bug, problem or improvement over priyanshudangipd@gmail.com
