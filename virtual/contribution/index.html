Virtual NIT-T

We are trying to convert our college(NIT Trichy) into a gamified model to give an awesome experience to the students who are missing the college during online semester.
Currently we've devloped a small portion and are planning to cover the entire campus, and maybe devlop our own insti game.
Excited?
Come On, Join us in making it real. Build the part of institute you love the most. We have used Blender to make models, and threeJS for rendering it over the site but will soon move over to Unity.
So, if you know Blender or Unity already, or even if you don't know but are willing to learn and contribute.
Connect with me over Instagram, or send a mail to priyanshudangipd@gmail.com.
Also, report any bug, problem or improvement over priyanshudangipd@gmail.com.
